# How to use Client website?
The client website (`thelocalcrafts.com`) is designed for maximum discovery and immersive shopping of authentic Pakistani handicrafts.

## 1. Exploring & Discovering Crafts
*   **Artisan Live-Feed**: On the homepage, you can scroll through a vertical video feed showing "Behind the Scenes" clips of artisans working in their workshops.
*   **Interactive Craft Map**: Navigate to the map section to explore Pakistan's heritage geographically. Tap on regions like **Multan**, **Hala**, or **Chiniot** to instantly see products from those areas.
*   **Semantic Search**: Our AI-powered search understands concepts. Instead of just keywords, try searching for "elegant wedding gifts", "traditional earthy pottery", or "hand-carved heritage furniture".

## 2. Immersive Product Experience
*   **Web-AR "View in Room"**: For high-ticket items like vases or furniture, tap the **"View in My Space"** button on your mobile device. This uses Augmented Reality to virtually place the product in your home to check size and style.
*   **AI Voice Narrations**: Every artisan story and product heritage description comes with high-quality AI audio. Click **"Listen to Story"** to hear the history of the craft while you browse.
*   **Digital Provenance**: Every item comes with a certificate of authenticity. You can scan the unique QR code on your product to view its origin, the maker’s profile, and the date it was crafted.

## 3. Impactful Shopping & Checkout
*   **Social Impact Fund**: During checkout, you have the option to add an **"Impact Tip"**. 100% of this goes directly to the artisan's fund for workshop tools and training apprentices.
*   **WhatsApp Checkout**: For a personalized experience, use the **"Order on WhatsApp"** button. This sends your cart details directly to our team for quick confirmation and support.
*   **Artisan Profiles**: Each product is linked to a deep profile where you can see the artisan's **Mastery Tier** (Apprentice, Artisan, or Master) and their total community impact.
