# How to use Artisan website?
The Artisan Panel is your digital workshop, designed to help you showcase your heritage and reach a global audience.

## 1. Building Your Digital Heritage
*   **Artisan Profile**: Set up your story. Describe your family's craft lineage and your workshop's history. This information is used by AI to build trust with global buyers (EEAT).
*   **Mastery Level**: Track your progress from **Apprentice** to **Master**. Higher levels give you better search placement, specialized badges, and lower platform fees.
*   **Impact Wallet**: View your **Impact Fund** balance. These are direct contributions from customers meant for buying new tools, raw materials, or training your next generation of apprentices.

## 2. Easy Product Management
*   **AI Listing Tool**: Don't worry about English descriptions. Upload a photo, record a short voice note in **Urdu** about the product, and our AI will create a professional, SEO-optimized English listing for you.
*   **Artisan Live-Feed**: Use your phone to record 15-30 second "Behind the Scenes" videos of your work. Upload them directly to appear on the homepage "Live-Feed" and show customers your authentic process.
*   **AR Model Requests**: For special items, you can request the Admin to create a 3D model for the **"View in Room"** AR feature.

## 3. Orders & Provenance
*   **Order Tracking**: Get instant notifications for new orders. Manage your shipping schedule and mark items as "Ready to Ship."
*   **Digital Provenance QR**: For every order, download the unique QR code. Print it and include it in your package. This tells the customer the exact history of the item they are holding.
*   **B2B Commissions**: Receive and respond to custom requests from designers through the B2B portal. Discuss custom dimensions and colors for bulk handmade orders.
