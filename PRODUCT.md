# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Customers (buying crafts), Artisans (selling crafts), Wholesale Partners (B2B bulk buying), and System Administrators (managing the platform).

## Product Purpose
A marketplace preserving South Asian heritage crafts by connecting artisans directly with global buyers, functioning as an e-commerce store, and serving as a portfolio and map of South Asian artisans and their work.

## Positioning
We partner directly with verified master artisans across South Asia to preserve heritage techniques, offering the largest catalog of traditional crafts and a seamless wholesale/retail platform.

## Brand Personality
Authentic, Heritage, Premium, Modern, Minimalist, Efficient, Vibrant, Cultural, and Accessible.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboards
- Cheap, mass-produced e-commerce sites
- Overly busy, chaotic layouts

## Design Principles
- **Practice what you preach:** Ensure the quality of the UI matches the premium craftsmanship of the heritage products.
- **Show, don't tell:** Highlight the artisans and their stories through high-quality imagery and clean layouts.
- **Expert confidence:** Maintain a modern, minimalist, and accessible interface that builds trust for both retail and wholesale buyers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High accessibility (WCAG AA, reduced motion support, high contrast) ensuring all users can navigate the platform seamlessly.
