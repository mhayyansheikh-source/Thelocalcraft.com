# How to use Admin website?
The Admin Panel (`admin.thelocalcrafts.com`) is the operational command center for the marketplace.

## 1. Governance & Moderation
*   **Artisan Approvals**: Review new artisan applications. Verify their documentation, heritage claims, and workshop locations before granting them "Approved" status.
*   **Mastery Tier Management**: Assign and review artisan tiers (Apprentice → Artisan → Master). Upgrade artisans based on their years of service and contribution to heritage preservation.
*   **Content Moderation**: Review all community submissions for the **Craftpedia Wiki** and artisan-uploaded "Live-Feed" videos to ensure high-quality standards.

## 2. Business Operations
*   **B2B / Commission Portal**: Manage inquiries from interior designers and corporate clients. Handle custom commission requests, bulk pricing negotiations, and tax-exempt documentation.
*   **Order Management**: Monitor marketplace-wide orders, track shipping status with global logistics partners, and manage payouts to artisans (including Impact Fund transfers).
*   **Revenue Tracking**: View consolidated reports on platform commissions, impact fund totals, and regional sales performance.

## 3. Technical & Security Control
*   **WAF & Security Dashboard**: Access the **BunkerWeb** and **CrowdSec** logs. Monitor and block malicious scrapers or bot attacks in real-time to protect artisan data.
*   **SEO Setup**: Manage the programmatic SEO engine, update metadata patterns, and monitor Google Search Console indexing for the 1,000+ regional silo pages.
*   **Analytics**: Utilize heatmaps to understand global customer interest and predict stock demands for specific craft regions.
