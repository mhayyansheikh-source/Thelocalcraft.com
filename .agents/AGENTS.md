# Agent Customizations

## Design Context
*This project uses the Impeccable skill for UI/UX design. See `PRODUCT.md` in the root for full details.*

- **Register**: Product (Web)
- **Purpose**: A marketplace preserving South Asian heritage crafts by connecting artisans directly with global buyers.
- **Positioning**: We partner directly with verified master artisans across South Asia to preserve heritage techniques, offering the largest catalog of traditional crafts and a seamless wholesale/retail platform.
- **Brand Personality**: Authentic, Heritage, Premium, Modern, Minimalist, Efficient, Vibrant, Cultural, and Accessible.
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS dashboards, cheap/mass-produced e-commerce sites, overly busy/chaotic layouts.
- **Design Principles**:
  1. **Practice what you preach:** Ensure the quality of the UI matches the premium craftsmanship of the heritage products.
  2. **Show, don't tell:** Highlight the artisans and their stories through high-quality imagery and clean layouts.
  3. **Expert confidence:** Maintain a modern, minimalist, and accessible interface that builds trust.
