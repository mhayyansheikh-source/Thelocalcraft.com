## Advanced Features & AI Integration

### 1. AI-Powered "Story to Product" Engine
Artisans can record rough voice notes in Urdu/Local languages. The AI (Llama 3 + Whisper) converts these into professional, SEO-optimized product descriptions in English, complete with craft history and family lineage details.

### 2. AEO & Semantic Search (pgvector)
Integration with Supabase pgvector allows for "Vibe Discovery." Users can search for "earthy living room vibes" or "heritage gift for heritage lovers," and the AI will return products based on semantic relevance (embeddings) rather than just keyword matches.

### 3. Digital Provenance (QR Codes)
Every product generates a unique QR code. When scanned by the customer, it reveals the "Digital Certificate of Authenticity," showing the artisan's profile, the date it was made, and a time-lapse video of the craft process.

### 4. Image Pipeline Optimization
Auto-classification of uploaded images. AI detects the craft type (e.g., "pottery") and automatically tags the product, suggest categories, and crops images for optimal display across mobile and desktop.

### 5. Multi-Currency & Global Logistics
Dynamic currency switching (PKR/USD/GBP/EUR/AED) based on user IP. Integrated shipping calculator for 20+ countries, factoring in fragile item handling and volumetric weight for international air freight.

### 6. Interactive "Craft Map"
A Web-GL based interactive map of Pakistan. Users can "fly" to different regions (Multan, Bhit Shah, Peshawar) to discover localized crafts through a visual, discovery-first interface.

### 7. Artisan Analytics Dashboard
Predictive analytics for artisans. The system analyzes global search trends (e.g., "Blue pottery is trending in UAE") and suggests which products they should craft more of to maximize earnings.

### 8. Enhanced Security & Anti-Scraping
Implementation of **BunkerWeb** as a WAF and **CrowdSec** for behavioral analysis to protect artisan data and unique photography from competitors and malicious scrapers.
