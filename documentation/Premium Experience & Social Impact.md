## Premium Experience & Social Impact

### 1. "Texture-Infused" UI Design System
Avoid flat, generic designs. Use CSS filters and SVGs to create "tactile" UI elements—buttons with clay-like gradients, cards with cloth-texture backgrounds, and loaders that look like spinning pottery wheels.

### 2. "Direct-to-Artisan" Social Impact Fund
A transparent tipping system at checkout. 100% of these "Impact Tips" go directly to the artisan's workshop for tool upgrades or apprentice training, tracked and displayed on the artisan's impact dashboard.

### 3. Immersive Commerce: Web-AR "View in Room"
Leveraging Google's Model-Viewer and WebXR. Customers can click "View in Room" on large items (furniture, large vases) to see exactly how the craft fits into their physical home using their smartphone camera.

### 4. Multimodal Stories: AI Voice Narration
Every artisan story can be "listened to." Using open-source Text-to-Speech (Piper), the system generates high-quality, professional narrations of the artisan's journey, making the heritage accessible even while browsing.

### 5. "The Craftpedia" - Collaborative Wiki
A community-driven encyclopedia for Pakistani crafts. Users can contribute historical facts, region-specific variations, and photos, creating a high-authority EDU/WIKI signal for search engines.

### 6. Artisan "Live-Feed" (Short-form Video)
A TikTok-style vertical video feed of artisans working in their workshops. High engagement "micro-moments" that build trust and social proof, with direct "Buy Now" overlays on the videos.

### 7. B2B / Wholesale "Commission" Portal
A dedicated flow for interior designers and corporate buyers to request custom, large-scale commissions directly from master artisans, bridging the gap between traditional craft and modern commercial projects.

### 8. Artisan "Mastery" Tiers
A gamified ranking system: **Apprentice → Artisan → Master**. Ranking is based on heritage preservation, apprenticeship training, and customer satisfaction, unlocking premium marketplace badges and lower commission rates.
